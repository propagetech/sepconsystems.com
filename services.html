Services

We at Sepcon Systems provide the following value additions and services as part of our commitment to protect the environment:
  • While undertaking Engineering Procurement & Construction Contracts we provide detailed design of structures and equipment their supply, supervision and erection. We partner with leading vendors and providers in order to deliver the guaranteed results.
  • As Turnkey Solutions & Systems providers, we provide our clients comprehensive engineering and project detailing and any further assistance, which is often beyond specified scope of works. We ensure our clients are well beyond satisfied while we stand by our guarantee of quality.
  • We provide 24/7 Operations and Maintenance services. We take up Service and Management Contracts that are customised to your requirements. We provide detailed O&M Training to the personnel in order to ensure smooth functioning of the treatment plant.
  • Other than providing the treatment units we help our customers in mitigating sludge and sludge disposal. We help you optimise or reduce the sludge production by following certain steps in adjusting bio-chemical reactions and providing training to the O&M personnel.
  • We are keen to partner with companies with similar ideals for Project Development & Co-investment.
  • We provide consulting services for project development featuring feasibility studies and preliminary designs and financial engineering. We also provide our consulting services for Rain Water Harvesting projects.
  • We provide ventilation and ducting system for treatment plant requirements. Apart from the field of water we also provide comprehensive Environment Protection services and consulting and also cater to Air Quality treatment requirements. We also provide air pollution control equipments.
Our host of services are available to ensure we provide comprehensive solutions. Contact us for a more detailed interaction.
