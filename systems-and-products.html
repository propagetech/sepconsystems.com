Systems & Products

The systems and products we offer are designed to specifically meet each of type of treatment requirement. While all our plants offer the following advantages, you can view our solutions specific to your segment of application.

Segments:

  • Industrial
  • Municipal
  • Residential and Commercial

The Sepcon Advantage:

  • Large scale EPC projects to smaller decentralised applications, we help all our clients achieve results while minimising cost of treatment.
  • Reliable systems with lower foot prints yet with high tolerance to changes in characteristics of feed water. They operate at higher flux rates and produce better quality of water.
  • Complete automation ensures the high quality of treated water. All the stages can be automated along with the right instrumentation. The process is also optimized for minimal energy utilization.
  • The team has developed a detailed understanding of various materials of construction, and configurations and hence optimise the systems and products for longer lifecycle. Multi-disciplinary expertise also ensures optimisation of civil works to reduce overall cost of the treatment plants .
  • Products are engineered and manufactured for guarantee of performance and long life. Equipments from the best brands are chosen to add to the guarantee of quality.
  • Closed circuit systems and promotion zero liquid discharge for industrial applications ensures protection of environment. Also, the effluent treatment plants can provide 90-95 % recovery for process reuse.
  • Provision of process water treatment plants with minimum wastage of intake water and bringing down consumption of fresh source water is both ecologically and economically advantageous.
  • Total Water Management and sludge handling ensures a comprehensive treatment.
